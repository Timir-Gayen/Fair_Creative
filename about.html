About Us

Welcome To Fair Creative!

More than printing...
Solution on Poly items

M/S Fair Creative was started Screen Printing on various items since 1986. Right from the beginning we aimed for the better to better quality. From the year 1990 we concentrated our effort to print plastic items through automation. We are the introducer of Acid alkali proof printing & four colour process job on poly bottles through silk screen printing process in EASTERN India in the year 1992.

In every 2 year span, we always focused to install the new machines with latest technology to develop the printing quality & to increase the production capacity.

Continuing Our Journey

We had installed our 4 colour Pad printing machine on 2006 to print on even or uneven surface such as CAP, COMB, CUP-PLATE, TOOTH-BRUSH, FLAT or OVAL BOTTLE, PEN etc. After that we centralised our concentration on Pad printing by installing few more Pad printing machines with latest technology & automation such as- Linear index, Oval index pad printing machines.

Now, we are concentrating on Dry offset printing process. We are the first manufacturer in India who has done multiple color printing direct on the balm containers instead of labelling which reduces the waste of time for labelling and reduces the cost also. We also achieved the security printing process with the help of this. With the help of Dry offset printing over the container, the product will have its identity and premium look for at-least 20 years. We can print over moulded Dishwash Containers,Food containers, Paint containers, Cosmetic containers, Ayurvedic containers, Medicine or Tablet packing containers which reduces the labour charge of labelling and saves more time.

Expanding Our Capabilities

On the basis of customer requirements, we have installed Batch Coding printing and synchronised it with online Automatic surface tension generating unit.

Also, We have installed Injection moulding machines with all the moulds by which we can mould packaging items, bottle caps, Automotive Parts & components, Game-toys, Pocket Combs, Storage Containers, Mechanical parts, Electrical equipments and many other Plastic available today.

From moulding to decoration by printing, Fair Creative has the complete solution of providing the best packaging. We have the ultimate solution of container decoration.
